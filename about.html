Story About Us

ārohaṇa

According to the ancient Indian Sanskrit texts, the word ārohaṇa (आरोहण) means to rise, to ascend or one who helps in rising or achieving the highest value of life (which in the Indian culture is liberation or moksha).

Through ārohaṇa management solutions, we intend to serve individuals and organizations in the areas of people management, design management, and project management. Our distinct approach and processes would produce results, which would take our clients to their next level and thus they would ascend in their performance, outlook, and realizations. This way we aim to contribute to individual and organizational excellence and well being.
